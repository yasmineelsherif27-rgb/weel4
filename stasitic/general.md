General Statistics
Introduction

Statistics is the science of collecting, organizing, analyzing, and interpreting data.

It helps us understand data and make better decisions based on evidence.

In Machine Learning, statistics is very important because it helps analyze datasets before building models.

Types of Statistics

Statistics is divided into two main types:

1. Descriptive Statistics

Descriptive statistics summarizes and describes the main features of a dataset.

Examples include:

Mean

Median

Mode

Range

Standard deviation

It helps us understand the general shape and distribution of data.

2. Inferential Statistics

Inferential statistics is used to make predictions or conclusions about a population based on a sample.

Example:

Using data from 100 students to estimate results for all students in a university.

Population and Sample
Population

Population refers to the entire group that we want to study.

Example:

All students in a university.

Sample

A sample is a subset of the population used for analysis.

Example:

A group of 100 students selected from the university.

Types of Data

Data can be divided into two types:

1. Qualitative Data

Describes qualities or categories.

Examples:

Gender

Color

Nationality

2. Quantitative Data

Numerical data that can be measured.

Examples:

Age

Height

Salary

Importance of Statistics in Machine Learning

Statistics helps in:

Understanding data

Detecting patterns

Making predictions

Evaluating models

It provides the mathematical foundation for Machine Learning algorithms
